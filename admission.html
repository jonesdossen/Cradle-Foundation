Thanks to our strong track record in delivering the English National and Cambridge curricular to our diverse student community, demand for a place at Ghana International School is highly competitive. As such, Ghanaians are encouraged to register at birth.

Parents may call 0302777163 and book an appointment to see the Head of Admissions or send in their enquiries via email at admissions@gis.edu.gh An Enquiry Form must be filled in after which an interview is carried out by the Head of Admissions. After the interview, an application form will be issued by the Head of Admissions if appropriate.

It is important to note that the Office of Admissions will only communicate with a parent or legal guardian. If a child is in the care of an adult who is not the parent, the Office of Admissions requires a letter of authorization from the parent or parents stating that the adult is able to act on their behalf in the affairs of the child.
